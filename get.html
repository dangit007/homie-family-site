Get Homie

Bring Homie to your family.

Homie is in a small, invite-only alpha. Pick your phone below to get set up — then open the app and choose New household to start your own.

iPhone & iPad

Install through TestFlight, Apple’s app for trying beta apps. First launch opens TestFlight, then Homie.

Install on iPhone

Needs an Apple ID aged 13+. Kids’ devices are set up by a parent.

Android

Android is in early testing — we add families one at a time. Email us and we’ll put you on the test and send your install link.

Request Android access

We’ll reply from a real person, usually within a day.
