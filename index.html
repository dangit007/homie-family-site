The mental load, lifted

Stop doing the math in your head at 10pm.

Every invitation sets off the same quiet math in your head. Who’s free, who can drive, what would have to move to make room. Homie runs that math for you, before you promise anyone anything.

Leo got invited to a birthday party Sunday at 2:00 PM, across town. Can we fit it?
Yes — but it’s tight. Maya’s soccer ends at 1:15 PM on the other side of town. Mom is free from 1:30 PM and could do the drop-off, arriving on time. Nothing else in the week moves.
Ok — put it in with Mom driving.
