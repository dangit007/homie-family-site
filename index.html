The mental load, lifted

Stop doing the math in your head at 10pm.

Every invitation kicks off the same silent calculation — who’s free, who’s driving, what has to move. Homie does that calculation for you, before you promise anyone anything.

Leo got invited to a birthday party Sunday at 2, across town. Can we fit it?
Yes — but it’s tight. Maya’s soccer ends at 1:15 on the other side of town. Mom is free from 1:30 and could do the drop-off, arriving on time. Nothing else in the week moves.
Ok — put it in with Mom driving.
